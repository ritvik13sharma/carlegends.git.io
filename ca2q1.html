The Car Legends are back with their choice of the 3 best👑 new SUV releases of India 2022 :

Mahindra XUV700 is an all new, authentic, global SUV set to redefine benchmarks with its sci-fi technology, spirited performance and first-in-class safety features and a great entering price point. The SUV is sure to attract Indian customers who are in seacrh of a 7-seater family SUV and an offroader. The SUV starts at Rs.13.68 lakhs and the top variant comes in at a whooping Rs.24.12 lakhs.
The new Safari builds on the legacy of prestige and outstanding performance; by combining Tata Motors' Impact 2.0 design language with the proven capability of Off-roading that Safari inherits, it makes a good 7-seater SUV with a Butch appeal and astonishing looks.
